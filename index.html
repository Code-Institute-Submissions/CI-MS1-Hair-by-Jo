About Me...

Hi, my name is Jo and I have been a professional hairdresser for over 23 years. I have been running my own business for the last 15 years and have a fully furnished home salon.


I excel in all areas of hairdressing and as a result, I am able to offer high quality services across all areas including hair cuts and colors, mens cuts, hair extensions, hair treatments and bridal.


I am a fully qualified hairdresser with NVQ level 1, 2 and 3 in Hairdressing, in addition to a D32 Diploma.


I have previously worked at Steven Way Hairdressing in Mayfair and also spent a significant amount of time working at D&J Ambrose in Pinner with five time British Hairdressing award winners.
